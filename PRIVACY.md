# Privacy Policy - Spectral Clip Forge

**Last Updated:** 2024

## Introduction

Spectral Clip Forge ("we", "our", "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data We Collect

### Uploaded Files
- **What:** Media files you upload for processing
- **Why:** To provide the video processing service
- **How Long:** Temporarily during processing, then deleted within 24-48 hours
- **Storage:** Locally on our servers (or cloud storage if configured)

### Processing Metadata
- **What:** File names, sizes, formats, processing options
- **Why:** To track jobs and provide service
- **How Long:** Stored in database, can be deleted on request

### Usage Data
- **What:** IP addresses, user agents, timestamps
- **Why:** Security, abuse prevention, rate limiting
- **How Long:** Logs retained for 30 days

### Optional User Accounts
If authentication is enabled:
- **What:** Username, email, password (hashed)
- **Why:** To provide user dashboard and history
- **How Long:** Until account deletion

## Data We Don't Collect

- We don't analyze or view your uploaded content
- We don't sell your data to third parties
- We don't use your content for AI training
- We don't track you across websites

## Data Security

- All processing happens in isolated containers
- Files are scanned for malware (if ClamAV enabled)
- Passwords are hashed with strong algorithms
- HTTPS encryption for all transfers (in production)
- Regular security updates

## Your Rights

- **Access:** Request your data
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your data and files
- **Portability:** Download your processed files
- **Opt-out:** Disable optional features

## Third-Party Services

We may use:
- Cloud hosting providers (AWS, DigitalOcean, etc.)
- Redis for job queuing
- No analytics or tracking services by default

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies:
- Session cookies for login (if auth enabled)
- No advertising or tracking cookies

## Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
